# Observability and Incident Readiness

## Goal

Ensure production failures can be detected, diagnosed, contained, recovered, and repaired.

Read [references/observability-incident-checklist.md](references/observability-incident-checklist.md) for detailed readiness criteria.

## Workflow

1. Identify critical paths, failure modes, dependencies, users, tenants, and business outcomes.
2. Define logs, metrics, traces, correlation identifiers, dashboards, and alerts.
3. Verify alerts are actionable, owned, tested, and tied to runbooks.
4. Verify degradation, kill switches, rollback, recovery, and data repair.
5. Exercise a realistic failure and record detection and recovery evidence.

## Output

Report critical signals, alert ownership, dashboards, runbooks, tested failure scenarios, containment, rollback, repair, gaps, and readiness decision.
